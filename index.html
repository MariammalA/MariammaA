A little
about me

I'm a UI/UX designer with 4 years of experience across fintech, food-tech, and service platforms — currently designing trading experiences at Samco Securities.

My path here was anything but straight: biotechnology, then fashion design, then design & technology. That mix is my edge — a scientist's curiosity, a designer's eye, and a storyteller's voice (I even taught spoken English once!). I care about products that are clear, usable, and genuinely nice to use, and lately I'm exploring AI-assisted workflows to move faster without losing the craft.
